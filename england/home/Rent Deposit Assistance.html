Help is available for people looking to rent a home in the private sector but do not have the deposit or rent in advance.

The scheme provides an interest free loan. It is for those who need a deposit to rent from a private landlord.

It is available to people who are homeless or threatened with homelessness and who would be accepted as homeless under homelessness legislation.

However, in exceptional circumstances it may be available to people who are not in priority need. In addition, a financial  assessment is made  to make sure you do not have a high level of debt.

The money will need to be repaid. This is done in affordable weekly or monthly instalments depending upon your financial status.

Homelink Rent Deposit Scheme

Homelink are an independent charity working in the Lewes District. They also run a rent deposit scheme.

This scheme was set up to help people who are not eligible for our scheme. To be eligible you must:

  • Be homeless or threatened with homelessness
  • Live in the Lewes District
  • Be on a low income or receiving benefit and not have enough savings or assets
  • Be able to demonstrate that you can live independently, manage finances and sustain a tenancy
  • Not have unmanageable outstanding debts

The scheme provides an interest free loan to cover the first months rent and damage deposit. You repay Homelink in affordable instalments.
